About Race Pace

Everything you need to know about Race Pace, and it's creator.

The History

Race Pace was built as a personal project by Michael McCarry. A Computer Engineer by trade and triathlete by heart, Michael put together his two skills to provide a tool for analizing training data to a higher degree. Race Pace was a solution for figuring out how to tell his loved ones just how long the bike leg of the race should take him.

He hopes others will benefit from this technology, and welcomes any feedback and ideas others may have to improve the helpfulness of this website.
